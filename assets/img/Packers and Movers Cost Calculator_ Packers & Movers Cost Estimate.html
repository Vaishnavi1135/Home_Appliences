A Guide to Packers and Movers Cost Estimates

Packers and movers price estimate is the tentative cost of moving services. Professional relocation companies provide the tentative cost of moving to their customers. This tentative relocation cost generally includes the cost of packing, loading, transportation, unloading, and unpacking.

It is always best to know the relocation charges in advance so that you can plan your moving budget accordingly. The packers and movers cost calculator is the best tool that can determine what amount you need to keep aside for your move. You must use this online tool for the cost estimation of your move. We recommend using this tool before collecting moving price estimates from different moving companies available in your neighborhood. This will give you a prior idea about the charges, and you can know if a company gives you an unrealistic high or low estimate. Therefore, knowing relocation charges before finalizing a company of packers and movers is imperative.

You must not go with a moving company that offers you the lowest price estimate. This can be a big scam. Shady packers and movers often use this technique to trap innocent customers. If you know the approximate movers and packers charges beforehand, you can also negotiate the rates and get the cheaper deal.

Cost heads in packers and movers estimate

A packers and movers estimate comprises of different cost heads. The most common cost heads in a moving price estimate are packing charge (inclusive of the cost of packing materials & moving boxes), labor charge (inclusive of charges for loading and unloading works), transportation charge (inclusive of the cost of fuel consumption in transit), and unpacking charge.

GST Charge & Other Taxes

Your estimate may also reflect the GST charge which is generally charged at the rate of 5% for transport only service and 18% for full-service packers and movers. For example, if the total cost of your move is Rs 10000, then the final cost including the GST charge will be Rs 11800 in the case of using full-service movers and packers.

If you are using a transport only service, then the final cost including the GST charge @ 5% will be Rs 10500. Other expenses can be Octroi, Toll Tax, and the cost of any additional or value-added service.

Insurance Charge

Insurance charges can be also included in the moving estimate if you are moving from one city to another. More often than not, professional packers and movers also offer insurance coverage for belongings they pack and move. Hence, you will also have to pay a nominal fee for moving insurance. Generally, there are two types of moving insurance - goods transit insurance and all-inclusive insurance.

You can avail goods transit insurance by paying a nominal fee, i.e., 1.5% of the declared value of goods. If you want to get all-inclusive insurance, then the insurance rate will be @ 3% of declared value. Additionally, you will also have to pay additional GST @18% on the insurance charge. So, it is imperative to know the price structure of packers and movers before hiring the required services.

How Do Movers and Packers Estimate Relocation Costs?

Professional packers and movers estimate relocation costs based on many factors. Different companies may have different parameters to estimate the charges. But the most common factors are as follows:

Distance between Point A and Point B

The distance between your current place and destination is the most important factor that relocation costs. If you employ movers and packers, then professionals will consider the distance to estimate the cost. The more distance movers will need to cover, the higher cost you will need to pay them. Usually, the distance does not affect the local shifting charges. But the distance is more than 15km, the movers may increase the charges.

Size & Type of Your Move

What is the type of your move? There can be different prices for home shifting, office shifting, local shifting, intercity shifting, industrial shifting, and car transportation. Moving companies will ask you the type of your move before giving you the price estimates. In addition, they will also ask you what the size of your move is. For example, 1 BHK, 2 BHK, 5 BHK, Small Office, Large Office, etc. The great size will attract higher prices.

Total Weight & Volume of Goods

The total weight and volume of goods is another important factor that hugely affects the relocation costs. Sometimes, movers and packers may not agree to estimate the charges based on the size of your property. They may want to know the actual weight and volume of goods before giving you the estimates.

For example, if there are items of 10 boxes to shift in a 2 BHK home and there are only 6 boxes to shift in a 5 BHK home, then the cost will be determined based on total volume & weight of items not based on the size of your move. The more items are required to ship the more cost you will have to pay. We recommend you get rid of old stuff and junk to reduce the weight and shifting cost as well.

Type of Items You Want to Shift

The type of items you want to shift may also impact the cost of hiring packers and movers. Professional packers and movers require different techniques and different types of packing supplies as per the nature of the items. Also, they may need different times and skills to handle two different types of items. Therefore, it is feasible that they will charge different prices for different types of items. If you have lots of fragile items to move, it is likely packers and movers charges will go high.

If you have furniture to move, movers and packers will give you an expensive estimate. It is because they will need to dismantle and re-assemble the furniture. They will also need more time and manpower. Therefore, the increased relocation fee is genuine.

Packing Materials

The total weight & volume of your belongings will be the deciding factors for the packing materials and boxes, movers will need to pack your belongings. The type and quantity of packing supplies may significantly influence the total relocation cost.

If you want premium packing for your belongings, movers and packers will estimate high cost for moving services. Any special packing will also attract a premium charge.

Suppose you are moving to a new city in the rainy season. It is likely that you will want waterproof packing for your goods. Waterproof packing supplies and boxes are expensive. Therefore, you will need to pay a premium fee in this case.

Other Factors

Apart from the above key factors, other factors can influence the movers and packers cost estimates. The season & timing of relocation, the floor of the residing building, the availability of lift facility, the experience of movers, the brand value of moving companies, the condition of road/route, insurance, GST, toll tax, Octroi, etc can also affect the relocation costs. It is always best to compare at least the top 3 packers and movers estimates. This will let you compare the quotes and go with the right one. Also, you need to ensure that the relocation estimate is given to you in writing. You must not accept the estimate over the phone call only.
